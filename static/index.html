About CrackSeg

CrackSeg is an AI tool built to check the structural health of concrete surfaces. It uses a custom deep learning model (U-Net) to find and highlight cracks down to the exact pixel.

How it scores damage

Instead of just measuring the total size of the cracks, the app calculates a Structural Integrity Score by looking at 5 things that actually matter for structural health:

  • Area: How much of the surface is covered?
  • Count: How many separate cracks are there?
  • Length: How far does the longest crack stretch?
  • Width: How wide are the cracks on average?
  • Branching: Do the cracks intersect into a dangerous web?
